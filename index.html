Bone / Slot Tree

Spine-style hierarchy: bone -> slot. Use + Bone to create default bones without slots. Slot label A = current active slot, B = this bone's selected slot. Drag slot row to reorder/assign bone, or drop to Unassigned/Staging area to keep it unbound. Right-click bone: delete bone and move slots to staging, or delete bone with its slots. Click S/B to fold, H toggles Work Hide on/off per bone, and use Hide Scope button to switch global scope (bone only / bone+slots). A sets Anim Hide key (bone+slots) in Pose mode.
